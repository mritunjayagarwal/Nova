What is

NOVA NETWORK?

Nova Network is an impressive all-in-one AI assistant that comes with a highly advanced backend model, while still maintaining a user-friendly simplified frontend design. Built on ChatGPT 4+ , Nova Network combines a variety of powerful utilities, allowing users to scan contract addresses, fetch real-time trading charts, and access a range of other tools and services, all completely free of charge. With its intuitive interface and powerful technology, Nova Network is the perfect tool for anyone seeking to simplify their digital life.
