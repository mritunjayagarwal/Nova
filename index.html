What is

Distributed Neural Orchestration?

Distributed Neural Orchestration (DxNO AI) is a framework that enables autonomous AI agents to collaborate dynamically through temporary neural connections called Neural Bridges. These connections facilitate real-time knowledge exchange and adaptive specialization, allowing agents to self-organize, evolve roles, and solve complex problems efficiently without central coordination.
